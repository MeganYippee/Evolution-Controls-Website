EVO/™ECM-DPC (Differential Air Pressure Control Unit)
  • An easy to use, cost-effective, one-piece air pressure controller. Control the air pressure in a space served by an EC driven fan or 0-10VDC controlled actuator. Great solution for variable volume building exhaust systems, room pressurization, and duct/plenum pressurization.

EVO/™ECM-SNV-S1 (Severe Environment Control Unit)
  • If you need a simple, rugged 0-+10V to ECM motor interface, the ECM-SNV Severe Environment control is the right choice. Small and simple to mount. Simply connect ~24V power, an input signal and two or three wires to the motor to start using the control. Mount the SNV in warm moist climates, or where cold weather taxes the life of other controls.
