EVO/™ECM-MODBUS (APPLICATION-SPECIFIC MODBUS RTU)

Allows an industrial or building automation system to monitor and control four EC motors over an industry-standard Modbus RTU serial data link.

Each EVO/ECM-MODBUS has four PWM or 0-10VDC control signals to control up to four EC motors.

Monitors RPM

Provides on/off control

Processes high and low RPM or contact alarms
