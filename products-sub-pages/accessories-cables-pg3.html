Cables



ECM-CBL-?? Cable Assembly

Cables are ordered by length (e.g. ECM-CBL-08 = Eight Foot Cable)

  • Available for use with EC Control Units.
  • Each cable comes with plenum rated cable material in custom lengths to fit your needs, a connector on one end that hooks directly into the EC Motor, and a connector on the other end that hooks to the EC Control Unit.

USM-CBL-?? Cable Assembly

Cables are ordered by length (e.g. USM-CBL-08 = Eight Foot Cable)

  • Available for use with EC Control Units.
  • Each cable comes with plenum rated cable material in custom lengths to fit your needs, a connector on one end that hooks directly into the EC Motor, and a connector on the other end that hooks to the EC Control Unit.
  • The motor connector is specifically for the NIDEC U.S. Motors brand.

ECM-PIG-?? Cable Assembly

Cables are ordered by length (e.g. ECM-PIG-08 = Eight Foot Cable)

  • Available to use with EC Control Units
  • Each cable comes with plenum rated cable material in custom length to fit your needs, a connector on one end that hooks to EC Control Unit and the other end is for making pigtail connection, for example, with wires from a Genteq 142R EC motor, see the wiring instruction in the TECH SUPPORT page.

ECM-PBL-?? Power Cable Assembly

Cables are ordered by length (e.g ECM-PBL-08 = Eight Foot Power Cable)

  • Each cable comes in lengths to fit your needs, a connector on one end that hooks directly into the ECM Motor (red jumper wire may be clipped out for 220V operation), and bare stripped wires on the other end to be hooked into power.
