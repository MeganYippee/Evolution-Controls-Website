EVO/™ECM-4Spd-PRO (4 Speed Interface Unit)

Allows 24 Vac controls to select one of four adjustable flow indexes for an EC Motor.

  • Fast or Gradual Speed Change.
  • Adjustment range is from Off to 100% of the programmed control range.

EVO/™ECM-DPC (Differential Air Pressure Control Unit)

The EVO/ECM-DPC controls an EC Motor powered fan or actuator to maintain an adjustable static or differential air pressure.

Set the air pressure setpoint to the desired pressure. Then proportional & integral control technology modulates the fan or actuator to maintain setpoint.

  • PWM or 0-+10V Output
  • Applications: Rooftop Exhaust, Cleanrooms, Ventilation Systems, Modulating Diffuser Systems, Restaurants.
  • Adjustment range is from Off to 100% of the programmed control range.
  • A fifth adjustment sets the flow index when no call is selected.
  • Pilot Pulse Enable/Disable.
  • PWM or 0-10 V output
